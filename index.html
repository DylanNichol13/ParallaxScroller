The Wall

'Night gathers, and now my watch begins.'

Standing seven hundred feet tall and stretching for three hundred miles, The Wall has safeguarded the Seven Kingdoms from the theats that lurk beyond. It was originally built by Brandon the Builder, with the assistance of the children of the forest and giants at the end of the Long Night.

Book your visit to The Wall today and explore the legendary structure, as well as the headquarters of the Night's Watch; Castle Black.
